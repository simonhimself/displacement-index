About

The Displacement Index is a public, data-driven dashboard that tracks whether AI-driven productivity gains are translating into broad prosperity — or showing early signs of labor displacement and downstream financial stress.

Principle

Data first. No trade calls. No narratives. Just measurement.

What it is

  • A structured set of indicators grouped into a causal chain
  • A transparent scoring methodology (z-scores over 5-year history)
  • A continuously updating snapshot, refreshed on a schedule

What it is not

  • Financial advice
  • A prediction market
  • A claim that any specific scenario will happen
