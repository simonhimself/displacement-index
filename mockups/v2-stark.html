Methodology

The Displacement Index tracks a specific economic hypothesis: that rapid AI capability gains could trigger a chain reaction through white-collar labor markets, consumer spending, credit markets, and housing. We track five sequential chain links, each scored against 5-year historical distributions using z-scores.

A reading above 0.5σ is elevated, above 1.0σ is warning, above 2.0σ is critical. The composite index maps the average chain link severity to a 0–100 scale. All data sourced from FRED and BLS. Credit spreads update daily; employment monthly; delinquencies quarterly.
